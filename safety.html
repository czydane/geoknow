Practical, easy-to-follow tips for staying safe before, during, and after natural hazards (volcanoes, earthquakes, landslides, and tsunamis). Print or save this checklist for quick reference.

  • Stay informed: subscribe to local alerts (SMS / radio / official social media) and know evacuation center locations.
